Why is this site here?

I made this site, initially as a side project to brush up on some basic HTML/CSS. Also, to ease the shame of being a technology professional without a personal website.

How did it go?

Hacky as hell. Initially just a few HTML pages before realising I may actually want to update this on the go without the tedium of managing directories and the likes. I then stumbled across Jekyll and it seemed to perfectly fit my needs. After a quick learning curve and much hack, I slowly got my head around it. I'll hopefully revisit this topic with a blog post.

What does it do?

Not much.

Now what?

There is much upheaval to occur in the near future. Geographically and professionally. I aim to use this blog to post some stories and snaps from my travels and perhaps a few blog posts on topics I find interesting as I go a new direction in my career.
